Choose Your Type


Standard

The original. Enjoy with or without cream and sugar.

Latte

A sweet drink made with espresso and hot steamed milk.

Cappuccino

Similar to the latte, this treat features espresso, hot steamed milk, and layer of foamed milk which sits perfectly on top.

Americano

This classy drink is a powerful but tasty infusion of a shot of espresso and water.

Espresso

The one-up of coffee. This drink is the strongest most basic choice yet it is a coffee enthusiast essential.

Macchiato

A mix familiar ingredients including espresso and steamed milk. Just like the latte minus the foamed milk.
